The government of India has announced a seven-day mourning to mark former
president APJ Abdul Kalam’s death on Monday. 
The official communication said, “The government of India announces 
with profound sorrow the death of Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, former President of India 
at Bethany Hospital, Shillong at 1945 hours, today. As a mark of respect to the departed
dignitary, seven days state mourning will be observed throughout India from 27 July 2015 
to 2 August 2015, both days inclusive.” 
A state funeral will also be held, but details have not yet been revealed.
